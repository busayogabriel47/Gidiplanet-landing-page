CWG at a glance

With over two decades ofimmense contribution to the information communication and technology sector, CWPG Plc has continuely remained a benchmark in Africa. The company commenced operation in Nigeria, on september 26th 1991 as Computer Warehouse Limited principally to cater for the hardware projects. Five years on, DCC Networks was established as the communication arm, to provide VSAT, Metropolitan, Wide Area and Local area Networks to corporate organizations. In order to concentrate on demands in the area of software soluotions, system and training, the Expert Edge software was acquired in 1999.

CWG Academy

The Academy was established to cater to the needs of IT Professionals within the ICT Industry in the locations where we operate. With over 1,550 Grandaunts since inception in 2010. The Academy's HQ is in Lekki, Lagos.
